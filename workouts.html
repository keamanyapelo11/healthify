Exercise should feel possible, not intimidating. You do not need a gym membership to improve your fitness. Start where you are and build gradually.

Beginner 10-Minute Workout

  • 20 Jumping Jacks
  • 10 Squats
  • 10 Push-ups (wall or floor)
  • 20 Mountain Climbers
  • 30-Second Plank Repeat 3 rounds.

Low Impact Workout

  • March in place – 1 minut
  • Chair squats – 15 reps
  • Step side to side – 1 minute
  • Wall push-ups – 10 reps Repeat 3 rounds.

Cardio Blast

  • High knees – 30 seconds
  • Rest – 30 seconds
  • Burpees – 10 reps
  • Rest – 30 seconds Repeat 4 rounds.

Core Routine

  • Crunches – 15 reps
  • Leg raises – 10 reps
  • Bicycle twists – 20 reps
  • Plank – 30 seconds Repeat 3 rounds.

Workout Tips

  • Warm up before starting.
  • Focus on correct form.
  • Rest when needed.
  • Stay hydrated.
  • Progress slowly each week.

Doing something small is always better than doing nothing.
